About Me - Vincent Moore

My name is Vincent Moore, and I am from the western part of Ghana. I've been happily married for two years now to my wonderful spouse, and we are blessed with a beautiful 11-month-old daughter named Zara. I currently work as a warehouse supervisor in a lighting shop.

I'm also pursuing my education, majoring in applied technology, and I'm working towards a Bachelor of Science (BS) degree. In my free time, I enjoy spending quality time with my family, and I believe that we are all here to support and get to know each other as a larger family. Let's connect and learn more about each other's stories and experiences.
